Welcome to DBHangOps!

DBHangOps is a bi-weekly google hangout meeting of various members of the MySQL community that simply want to talk shop about MySQL in their day-to-day and share fun learnings from working with MySQL and other data stores. Also be sure to check out #hangops, which was part of the original inspiration for DBHangOps!

When's this?

DBHangOps happens every other Wednesday at 12:00pm Pacific (UTC -8). You can follow the @DBHangOps twitter account to get up-to-date information about the next session, the link for the google hangout, and other interesting retweets!

What do a bunch of DBAs talk about..?

Topics are always suggested by anyone attending the google hangout or via e-mail and twitter. If you have an idea for a topic that you'd like to see, definitely let us know! If you're curious about previous topics, be sure to checkout the previous recordings on the #DBHangOps youtube playlist.
